Our ‘Business-Candymaker’:

The business team is led by Vishal Ahluwalia.
A Senior Vice President with over 25 years of experience of leading teams in Indian and international market. Vishal has held leadership roles in advertising agencies like Lowe, JWT, Contract, TBWA, Grey, and Cheil. He is the brain behind the biggest automobile brand launch in Indian market - MG Hector and ITC B’ Natural Juices launch in 2015 amongst many others. In Candy Shop Communications, he brings his expertise to launch, create and sustain brands. His fresh, bold perspective on business and its understanding promises growth marketing in a cost effective way.

Our ‘Strategy-Candymaker’:

The strategy team is led by Archana Singh. Micro-influencer. Marcom strategy specialist. Public Speaker. India’s most awarded Travel Blogger. Currently, along with her own venture Travel See Write, she is heading the strategy division at Candy Shop Communications where using her rich Marcom Experience and Micro-Influencer abilities she is helping brands achieve their marketing and communication goals. She was also associated with Starcom MediaVest Group as Connections Director on the entire Coca-Cola portfolio in the Philippines.
